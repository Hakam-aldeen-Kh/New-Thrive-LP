Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about thriving in Japan

What is the Thrive in Japan program?

Thrive in Japan program includes a groundbreaking and original curriculum, unlimited speaking sessions and access to an active community who is committed to helping you thrive in Japan.

What if I already have JLPT but I still want to learn to Thrive?

To guarantee that we are creating the type of supportive learning community students need for success we are releasing the program in stages. Currently Thrive in Japan is only available for Absolute beginners and N5 level students.
N4 curriculum will be released in November 2025.
N3, N2 and N1 programs have planned release dates in 2026.

Who can benefit from the Thrive in Japan program?

Simply put: anyone serious about learning Japanese Language and Culture. If you are currently struggling in Japan, this is for you. If you are planning on coming to Japan, Thrive in Japan will easily shave years off of your learning curve.

What if I speak advanced Japanese? What if I'm a beginner?

At the moment Thrive in Japan is only accepting true beginners or N5 level learners. We will be launching more advanced levels in stages starting in November 2025 with N4, and finishing with N1 at the end of 2026.

Do you actually teach Japanese Language?

Yes, we do, thrive in Japan is a Japanese Language program that leaves other programs in the dust. BUT before we start teaching you the language we encourage that ALL of our students go through "The Paradigm Shift" Course.
Japanese is at least 60% unspoken communication skills, tools and strategies. The remaining 40% is made up of words and grammar structures.
